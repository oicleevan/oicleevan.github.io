June 11th â€” Pixies - Surfer Rosa

One of my favourites--a dirty, weird, and influential album with some cool ideas. Some really interesting ideas were presented on this debut full-length. Mixing punk, surf, and Captain Beefheart together for a crazy good album. Would prove incredibly influential to the alternative rock scene of the late 80s and early 90s, and I can see why. Whether it's the noisy diversion of Vamos, the reverberating roar of Where Is My Mind, or the ode to incest that is Broken Face, there's some crazy stuff going on. Powerful production from Steve Albini makes for a great sounding album too, especially the drums. WOW!

My favourite tracks: Something Against You, River Euphrates, Where Is My Mind, Vamos
