black midi - Hellfire

black midi leave post-punk behind, and in the process, create one of the most interesting and exciting albums of the last decade. Very few moments go to waste throughout this project, and the band weaves jazz, rock, and theatrics together to a powerful end.

My favourite tracks: Sugar/Tzu, Welcome to Hell, Dangerous Liasons, 27 Questions
